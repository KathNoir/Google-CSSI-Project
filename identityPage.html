Identity Theft

Identity theft is a cybercrime where a group or an individual obtains the personal information of another and poses as them or uses their bank information to make purchases in the victimâ€™s name. This can result in a diminishing of funds as well as intense panic for the victim. These types of frauds are often executed through call, text, or email.

IF THE OFFER IS:

1) Asking for bank account information
2) Causing unrecognized activity in your financial reports
3) Requesting money or personal information
BEWARE OF MALICIOUS CONTENT

WHAT TO DO TO AVOID IDENTITY THEFT:

1) Download anti-virus software
2) Do not provide bank information to disreputable receivers
3) Check your credit and debit information often
4) Create new passwords multiple times per year
