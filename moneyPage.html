Money Mule Scams

Money mule scams create false situations in order to incentivize and persuade individuals to assist them in transferring stolen money or transferring their own money. This can be done through call, email, or text, and often frames the victim as the perpetrator and causes them legal trouble if they unknowingly moved stolen funds. The scammers commonly tell the individual to transfer this money via gift cards, mail, or wire transfer.

IF THE OFFER IS:

1) Money requested through gift cards
2) Money requested through cash via mail
3) Money requested through wire transfer
BEWARE OF MALICIOUS CONTENT

WHAT TO DO TO AVOID MONEY MULES:

1) Only utilize trusted online transfer systems
2) Do not send money to an online friend/partner
3) Never pay to collect a prize
4) Report any suspicious activity to the Federal Trade Commission
