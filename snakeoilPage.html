Snake Oil Solutions

Snake oil solutions are products or information that claim to be able to cure conditions such as deafness, ADHD, autism, and others similar. They entice individuals into spending money to obtain such items while, in actuality, they are not working remedies to any such ailment. They can be spread as misinformation through websites and emails.

IF THE OFFER IS:

1) Not scientifically supported
2) From an unreputable seller
3) From an unreputable seller
BEWARE OF MALICIOUS CONTENT

WHAT TO DO TO AVOID SNAKE OIL SOLUTIONS:

1) Ignore any suspicious messages
2) Ask your healthcare provider
3) Consult scientific research
