ABOUT US

Our goal is to distribute information regarding various types of online scams to those who may not know such details. In the modern age of the Internet where technology is constantly evolving, it is imperative to be aware of the concurrent rise in cybercriminal activity as well as how to properly inhibit it.

WHY IT'S IMPORTANT

Understanding what scams exist is the first step to avoiding them. Without this knowledge, an individual would likely become increasingly susceptible to the risks of having their personal information taken from them and utilized for illegal or unapproved acts. In order to prevent this theft from occurring, one must learn what to look out for and the proper actions to take in case they find themselves in such a situation.
